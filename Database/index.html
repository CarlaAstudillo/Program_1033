New Jersey Program 1033

Search our database to see which law enforcement agencies received military weapons and equipment from the Department of Defense's Program 1033. We received the database from the Department Of Defense's Defense Logistics Agency (DLA). General items such as office equipment, flashlights and boots are detailed to the local law enforcement level. However, when it comes to tactical items such as armored trucks, night vision goggles and assault rifles, DLA would only give us which counties received the equipment. This is an active inventory list, which means that DLA tracks the equipment for as long as the agency has it. However, when it comes to general non-tactical items like office supplies, the DLA stops tracking it after a year. Download raw data here.
